Paris, France

About Paris

Paris, the City of Light and the capital of France, is a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Its picturesque 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine. Beyond such landmarks as the Eiffel Tower and the 12th-century, Gothic Notre-Dame cathedral, the city is known for its cafe culture and designer boutiques along the Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré.

Must-See Attractions

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • Notre-Dame Cathedral
  • Champs-Élysées
  • Sacré-Cœur Basilica

Local Cuisine

Paris is a food lover's paradise, with an array of local dishes and international cuisine to savor. Some must-try Parisian dishes include:

  • Baguettes
  • Croissants
  • Escargot
  • Coq au Vin
  • Crème Brûlée
